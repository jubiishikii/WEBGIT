Filipino Favorites

Adobo

Description: Chicken or pork braised in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves.

Ingredients: Chicken or pork, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, black pepper, oil.

Cooking Style: Slow-cooked until tender and flavorful, with the sauce reduced to a savory glaze.

Serving Suggestion: Best enjoyed with steamed white rice and a side of pickled vegetables or a hard-boiled egg.
