Sinigang

Kare-Kare

Description: A rich and creamy peanut stew traditionally made with oxtail, tripe, and a mix of vegetables.

Ingredients: Oxtail, tripe, peanut butter, ground rice, eggplant, string beans, banana flower, annatto, garlic, onions.

Cooking Style: Slowly simmered until tender, thickened with peanut and rice, and typically served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste).

Serving Suggestion: Served hot with rice and bagoong on the side for added umami.
