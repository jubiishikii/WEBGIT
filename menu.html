Filipino Favorites

Adobo

Chicken or pork braised in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves.

Sinigang

A tangy tamarind soup with pork, shrimp, or fish and mixed vegetables.

Kare-Kare

Rich peanut stew with oxtail, tripe, and assorted vegetables.

Lechon Kawali

Crispy deep-fried pork belly served with liver sauce.
