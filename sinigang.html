Adobo

Sinigang

Description: A tangy tamarind soup with pork, shrimp, or fish and mixed vegetables.

Ingredients: Tamarind, pork or shrimp or fish, tomatoes, onions, water spinach (kangkong), eggplant, string beans, radish.

Cooking Style: Simmered with tamarind base until meat is tender and vegetables are cooked through, resulting in a sour and savory broth.

Serving Suggestion: Serve hot with steamed rice, and optionally with fish sauce or chili on the side.
